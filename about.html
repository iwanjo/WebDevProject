FAQS

Frequently Asked Question

Does AfricaExplored have family trips?

AfricaExplored offers a wide range of family trips around Africa. The minimum age does depend on the destination of travel, however if in the company of a legal guardian, an underaged child will be able to go.

What are the best airport hacks?

Download the GateGuru app before your next trip: It shows maps and amenities for any terminal, so you can better plan where to eat lunch or whether you can arrive early for a quick manicure.

I have a disability - can I travel with AfricaExplored?

At AfricaExplored we are committed to ensuring that no-one loses their sense of wonder. We aim to make travel widely accessible to people of all walks of life, regardless of ability or disability. We do however ask that you carefully consider whether the trip will be right for you given the physical or mental burden of the destination. Many travellers with us who are worried about this, enjoy their trips by travelling with a friend or companion who can assist them at any point of their trip.

Are AfricaExplored trips suitable or LGBTQI travellers?

No politics here. Everyone, and we mean everyone, no matter how one chooses to identify, will be treated equally by AfricaExplored, and will feel comfortable when they travel with us. Do I need travel insurance?

This is mandatory for all trips on AfricaExplored, and should be applied for on the time of booking with your insurance provider.

How do I organise my Visa?

AfricaExplored does not assist with visa applications, for support on this please contact your local embassy to organise your visa to your chosen destination.
